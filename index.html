About Leo XIV

Pope Leo XIV (formerly Robert Prevost) was elected as the Bishop of Rome on May 8th 2025 following the death of Pope Francis. Unique for being the first American to be elected to the office of Pope, almost no one considered Cardinal Robert Prevost as a possible candidate. He was born in Chicago but served much of his ministry in Peru as an Augustinian.
