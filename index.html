Jesus Christ

Who is Jesus of Nazareth?

As Christains we believe:

  1. He is the Only Begotten Son of God
  2. He was born of the Father before all ages
  3. He is consubstantial with the Father
  4. For our salvation; by the Holy Ghost He was incarnate of the Virgin Mary and became man
  5. He was crucified under Pontius Pilate, died, and was buried
  6. He rose again on the third day
  7. He ascended into heaven and is seated at the right hand of the Father
  8. He will come again in glory
  9. He will judge the living and the dead
  10. His kingdom will have no end

Pope Leo XIV

2025-Present

About Leo XIV

Pope Leo XIV (formerly Robert Prevost) was elected as the Bishop of Rome on May 8th 2025 following the death of Pope Francis. Unique for being the first American to be elected to the office of Pope, almost no one considered Cardinal Robert Prevost as a possible candidate. He was born in Chicago but served much of his ministry in Peru as an Augustinian.
